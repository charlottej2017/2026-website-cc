FAQs

Common questions

How long does a project take?

Most website projects are completed in 1–3 weeks depending on the scope. Brand identity packages typically take 1–2 weeks. Timelines are confirmed after our discovery call.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to secure your project spot. The remaining balance is due upon completion before files are delivered.

What platforms do you work with?

I primarily work with Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, and more. If you're on a different platform, reach out and we can discuss options.

How many revisions are included?

Each service package includes a set number of revision rounds, outlined in your project proposal. Additional revisions are available at an hourly rate.
